During grinding work under an active hot work permit at B1-O03, the area CO monitor briefly registered a reading above the 50 ppm alert threshold. The permit holder stopped work immediately and the area was cleared as a precaution. Follow-up monitoring showed CO levels back within normal range within four minutes, consistent with a brief puff from a nearby charging operation rather than a sustained leak. No injuries occurred and no equipment was damaged.

Resolution / Corrective action: Work resumed after two consecutive clear readings; ventilation fan positioning adjusted for future permits in this bay.
